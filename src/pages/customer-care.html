// HOW TO CARE FOR YOUR ACCESSORIES?

Just like how you care for your loved ones! 



  • Avoid wearing your accessories to showers; avoid contact with liquids including water, perfume and oil.
  • Use a slightly damp cloth/wet wipe to gently clean accessories after use.
  • Store your accessories in a dry environment and away from the sunny sun!
  • Avoid using excessive force when removing your accessories as this may cause wear and tear.
  • Handle your accessories with care as certain accessories may be more fragile than others when dropped.


In summary, just protect your accessories with your life.
