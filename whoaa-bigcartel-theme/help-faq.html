    Who are you?

    Thank you for your interest, I get this a lot (hehe)! I am a fellow Singapore citizen, old-soul at heart and a earring connoisseur. I am a self-proclaimed patriotic Singaporean auntie who loves a good bowl of bakchormee and everything local (explains some of the local twist to my earrings)! Slide into my DM and we can talk about everything under the sun!


    Is this a full-time hustle? How was whoaa! started?

    Nope, it isn't! whoaa! is a passion project/side hustle; I hold a day job in the Marketing industry! For those wondering, whoaa! was started on 9th May 2020 as a result of Circuit Breaker (Singapore's version of lockdown for COVID-19). I have always dreamt of having my own accessory shop – just because quirky and out of the norm earrings give me so much happiness and I hope to spread this same happiness to other individuals. I started this little venture during Circuit Breaker right after my last official university assignment and when everything was gloomy. At that point in time, I convinced myself to get started (been thinking for the longest time) given how life was so unpredictable and if I don't get started now, I probably would regret – e.g. how COVID-19 was so rampant and anyone could be the next victim. And especially since I could run this little venture entirely online. & here you go, whoaa! Be prepared, we will be here for a long time!
